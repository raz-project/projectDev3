 1. Pod status in all namespaces

kube_pod_status_phase{phase=~"Pending|Running|Succeeded|Failed|Unknown"}

This shows the status (0 or 1) for each pod in every namespace.
To summarize counts by status and namespace:

sum by (namespace, phase) (
  kube_pod_status_phase{phase=~"Pending|Running|Succeeded|Failed|Unknown"}
)
